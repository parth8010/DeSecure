================================================================================
RISK MITIGATION
================================================================================

Technical Risks:
- Quantum computing advancement faster than expected
  → Stay updated with NIST standards, implement hybrid crypto

- AI model accuracy issues
  → Continuous retraining, multiple model ensemble, human review option

- Scalability challenges
  → Microservices architecture, horizontal scaling, CDN usage

- EEG hardware compatibility
  → Support multiple devices, provide fallback authentication


Business Risks:
- Regulatory compliance changes
  → Legal consultation, flexible architecture, compliance monitoring

- Competition from established players
  → Focus on unique features, open-source community, better UX

- User adoption challenges
  → Freemium model, excellent documentation, community building
